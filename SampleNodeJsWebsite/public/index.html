Welcome to Our Community

OUR PROCLAMATION

"They devoted themselves to Apostles' teaching and to fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to prayer." Acts 2:42

We, as the people of God, our mission is to worship God, to nurture people on their spiritual journey, and to witness to God's love in word and deed.

Relying on the grace and power of God, known to us most fully in the life and teaching of Jesus Christ, we aspire to be a community which is biblically based, intellectually open, theologically progressive, and inclusive of all people.

Fostering an atmosphere of joy and celebration that welcomes all people, we commit to provide:

  • Varied and meaningful opportunities for worship and spiritual growth.
  • Engaging and interactive education for all age groups.
  • A caring and compassionate ministry to members and friends of the community.
  • Loving service to the wider community with a focus on human need, social justice, and stewardship.
  • Generous material and spiritual support for our mission and community.
